Our Story

Ever since we opened in 1969, Michael's Grilled Food has been reliably serving everyone in town. Our founder, Stuart McEvan, had a dream to put an end to artifical flavors in foods. And ever since that day in 1969, we've been working to server all of Louisiana, and one day the whole world.

When you come to Michael's Grilled Food on Paul Wayne Haggerty Road, you can expect only the best service, quality, staff, and expireince due to our dedicated team always trying to make the customer statisfied whenever they come to Michael's Grilled Food to eat the highest quality grilled food in all of Louisiana.
